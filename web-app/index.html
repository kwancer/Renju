Game Rules

Renju is played with the following rules:

The goal is to get five stones in a row, while preventing your opponent from doing the same.

In this version of the game you may use the mouse or the arrow keys and enter to place stones.

You may choose your game settings below. Classic is a standard 15x15 board.

Please also ensure you are familiar with the rules of the game.
